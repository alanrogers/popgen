Optional Readings

Felsenstein, Joe. Theoretical Evolutionary Genetics
An excellent text, but more advanced than the one I use for this course.
More on Python
You should not need any additional text on Python. But if you find that you do, here are a couple of choices:
  1. Byte of Python
  2. Python Tutorial by Guido van Rossum.
Linkage disequilibrium
If you find Gillespie's treatment of this subject a little cryptic, then try Crow and Kimura or Felsenstein (pages 31-34).
Wang et al
Surveys the human genome for evidence of ongoing selective sweeps. Finds 1800 loci under selection.
