In the list below, the numbers indicate lecture topics. Click on one, and you'll get the slides I showed in class. Lectures are assigned on dates listed in the syllabus. More than one lecture may be assigned on a given date. The dates in the list below tell you when the lecture slides were added to this website. Under each lecture topic, the open circles indicate additional materials: the YouTube entries are links to videos of the lectures. The other entries are reading assignments.
